About Me

Hello! My name is Nadia Belghozlane.

I am a recent software development graduate from Eleven Fifty Academy in Indianapolis, IN (May 2021). I have a short, but strong history in coding. Before Eleven Fifty, I attended Purdue University Northwest/Fort Wayne and received my Bachelor of Science degree in Communication Sciences and Disorders in December 2016. In the last six months, I’ve gained over 500 hours of coding experience and learning time.
